REIGNITE Design Bible

Core Palette

  • Navy #1B2A4A — structural base, trust, authority.
  • Navy Dark #0F1A2E — high-contrast anchors and depth.
  • Orange #CC6C05 — primary accent and action color.
  • Orange Ink #B15F19 — hover/depth accent.
  • Orange Soft #F2B36F — supporting highlight.

Typography

  • Primary family: Plus Jakarta Sans
  • Headline behavior: short, decisive, high-contrast
  • Body behavior: compact paragraphs, high scanability

Component Rules

  • CTAs use orange background with white text.
  • Cards use white background with subtle navy borders.
  • Diagrams use navy structure + orange directional accents.
  • Avoid over-animation in core conversion flow.

Visual Do / Don’t

  • Do: clean geometry, centered alignment, restrained effects.
  • Do: use visuals to explain architecture, not decorate.
  • Don’t: drift into generic finance-site blue/gold feel.
  • Don’t: overload with repeated motifs that dilute meaning.
