Frequently Asked

Common questions about working with Reignite Advisors.

Who is REIGNITE for?

REIGNITE is for experienced professionals with 10+ years of expertise who are ready to monetize their knowledge. You should have deep experience in your field and be willing to package it into a structured offer. This is not for those looking for quick fixes — it's for professionals ready to build a real, scalable asset.

Do I need a business idea already?

No. You bring the experience — we bring the structure. Many of our clients have expertise but no clear business idea. We help you identify what you've actually built and how to package it. You don't need a plan; you need experience and willingness to do the work.

Is this a course?

No. REIGNITE is a private architecture engagement — not a course, not a group program. You get 1-on-1 strategic advisory throughout the entire process. We build your asset with you, not just teach you how to do it.

How long is the engagement?

The core engagement is designed to deliver clarity within 30 days and a market-ready offer within 90 days. However, the exact timeline depends on your starting position and the complexity of your expertise. Strategic advisory continues throughout to ensure your asset evolves with market opportunities.

What do I leave with?

You leave with a clearly defined intellectual asset, a precise audience willing to pay, a structured sellable offer, and premium-aligned positioning. You'll have a repeatable system that converts expertise into revenue and scales beyond your time.
