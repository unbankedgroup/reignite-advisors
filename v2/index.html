What is the baseline requirement for the fit check?

This infrastructure is exclusively for operators with a minimum of 10 years of validated field execution. If your bottleneck is a lack of experience, this is not for you. If your bottleneck is a lack of structure around massive experience, this is exactly for you.

What is the structural output of this engagement?

You exit with a codified Intellectual Asset Architecture. This includes your extracted IP, a premium fixed-fee offer design, and a repeatable leverage system that does not rely on your constant presence.

How is this different from a growth agency?

Agencies execute marketing on top of your existing, unstructured offers. We do not amplify weak architecture. We build the structural asset first, ensuring your market signal and pricing power are established before you scale.

How long is the engagement?

The core engagement is designed to produce foundational architecture outputs within 30 days, and a market-ready flagship offer within 90 days. Exact timing depends on your starting position and operational complexity.

I’m already at capacity. How much of my time does the 'Architecture' process require?

Architecture is an extraction process, not a 'study hall.' We don't add to your plate; we codify what is already on it. While we require your deep field judgment for the extraction phase, the heavy lifting of documentation and structural mapping is handled by the REIGNITE team.

Is this for people starting a new business or scaling an existing one?

Neither. This is for established operators who have already achieved results but realize their success is presence-dependent. We don't build businesses; we build the intellectual assets that allow an existing track record to scale without your constant live involvement.

Is there a guarantee?

We do not offer 'money-back' marketing gimmicks. We offer structural integrity. The Fit Check exists specifically to ensure your lived judgment is solid enough to be codified. If you are accepted, it is because we have determined your expertise can be transformed into a permanent, priced asset.
