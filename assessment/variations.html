Revenue is flowing, but it requires your direct involvement at every step. The engine works — it just doesn't scale. With architectural work, you could generate revenue from assets instead of hours.

Scale Readiness — Leverage + Direction (7/10)

This is your strongest dimension. The vision is there. The direction is clear. What's missing isn't strategy — it's structure. Once the architecture catches up to your ambition, scale becomes inevitable.
